ABOUT US

A new level of elegance and comfort

Sit comfortably on the sheets and enjoy the beautiful interior of the Athen Elegance model. A perfect blend of contemporary, stylish interior finishes and high-quality amenities to create a luxurious interior. The seats are finished with 'Napa' leather to add luxury, while ergonomically designed front seats are equipped with electric seats including ventilation seats and lumbar spine to provide a comfortable and stable seating experience.

Sufficient headroom and legroom are available for both front and rear seats, providing comfort and relaxation on all journeys with Athen. The frameless window features a typical style element of the Grand Touror, with a rear window and a large up-open tailgate making it easier to use the leisurely and affordable space.

Lane Assist

Lane assist is a lane keeping system that helps prevent accidents caused by involuntary deviations. If any movement of the vehicle is detected out of the lane, a prompt warning is given to the driver to assist in safer and more comfortable driving.
Adaptive Cruise Control is a smart safety system that controls the vehicle speed and maintains a set distance to match the speed of the vehicle ahead, whether in the city or on the highway.
